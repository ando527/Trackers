In the heart of the Australian Alps, and on the rooftop of the country, Falls Creek is a place where nature, culture and adventure combine to provide a unique experience. With temperatures averaging 10 degrees cooler than surrounding valleys a trip to the mountains is a great way to escape the summer heat. At Trackers we can cater for work retreats, conferences and summer weddings as well as a relaxing break.

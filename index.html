Built in 1977 this classic Australian ski lodge has long term owners and is led by lodge manager Brian Henning and a dedicated team, the majority of whom return year on year and love sharing all of the fun our village has to offer with our guests. The lodge offers a mix of 15 rooms, our standard rooms give you access to all the great lodge facilities whilst our panorama and spa panorama rooms are slightly larger and offer stunning mountain views. Your peak snow season accommodation tariff is a great value alternative and includes breakfasts, afternoon teas, dinners, all day tea and coffee, kids clubs, Xmas in July and all lodge based activities.

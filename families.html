We think bringing your children to the snow is not just a holiday, it is an investment. Most people can remember the first time they encountered snow and we go the extra mile to make sure your children leave our lodge with treasured memories they will look back on for years to come. Kids get the royal treatment at Trackers. We have a special early meal for them (if they chose) and then they get to relax in kids club with a babysitter whilst their parents can unwind over their dinner. Santa visits on Wednesday with presents for them and there are plenty of toys, video games and toboggans for them to have fun with during their stay. Through the week we have Meet your Instructor nights. These nights provide you with an opportunity to have a drink and a meal with an experienced Falls Creek instructor so you can get an insiders view of the mountain. make sure not to miss our 80s theme nights or Mimosa Mondays.
